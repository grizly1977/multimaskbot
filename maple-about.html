Our Vision

The Self-Service Vision

The enterprise has traditionally operated through manual handoffs, siloed tools, and fragmented workflows. Teams spend more time searching for capabilities, waiting for access, and navigating bureaucracy than doing the work that matters.

MAPLE reimagines this experience — a single, unified command center where every team member, regardless of technical skill level, can discover what the enterprise offers, access tools on demand, and execute complex workflows without bottlenecks.

By combining intelligent capability mapping, AI-powered insight services, and guided self-service workflows, MAPLE transforms the enterprise from a maze of disconnected systems into a cohesive, empowering experience.

Key Benefits
Eliminate Friction

No more ticket queues or waiting for access. Teams can explore, configure, and run services themselves.

Surface Knowledge

Accelerate Delivery
